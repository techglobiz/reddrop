About RedDrops

Founded in 2022, RedDrops is India’s largest youth-led organization working towards 100% blood sufficiency. With thousands of volunteers nationwide, we have transformed blood donation from an emergency necessity into a culture of regular contribution.

  • Organizing regular blood donation camps across the country
  • Maintaining a 24/7 helpline for urgent requests
  • Educating communities to break myths about donation
  • Partnering with hospitals, blood banks, and government agencies

Our Vision

To see a nation where no life is lost due to the lack of blood. We aim to create a culture where blood donation is as routine as voting — an act of civic responsibility and love.

Our Mission

To connect donors and patients instantly through technology, organize camps nationwide, and build an empowered community of youth leaders championing voluntary donation.

How We Work

1. Awareness

We run campaigns in colleges, offices, and communities to spread accurate information and inspire donations.

2. Connection

Using our helpline and database, we match urgent blood requests with nearby donors instantly.

3. Support

Our volunteers assist at donation camps, ensure safe practices, and provide emotional support to families.
